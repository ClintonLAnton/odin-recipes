Strawberry Mimosa

Simple Homemade Strawberry Mimosa

Quick 5 min recipe for a delightful strawberry mimosa to compliment your Sunday brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 chopped strawberry, or more if needed
  • 1 tbsp. sugar
  • 1 bottle of champagne, plus more for topping off flute (glass)
  • Sliced strawberries, for garnish

Steps

  1. In a blender or food processor, blend strawberries, sugar, and champagne until fully combined, then pour into champagne flutes and top with more bubbly.
  2. Garnish with a strawberry slice and serve.
