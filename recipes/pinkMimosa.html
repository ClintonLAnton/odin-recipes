Simple Homemade Pink Mimosa

Quick 5 min recipe for a delightful pink mimosa to compliment your Sunday brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 bottle of tropicana farmstand strawberry banana
  • 1 bottle of champagne
  • Ice Cubes

Steps

  1. In a champagne flue or drinking class add two ice cubes. Then pour in 2 oz of Tropicana Farmstand Strawberry Banana, and on top of that 2 oz of champagne, stir once with straw and enjoy!
